## Why anon.li?

Most privacy tools are either too complex for everyday use or don't go far enough. We set out to change that by building a service that is:

- **Simple**: A modern, clean interface that anyone can use
- **Secure**: Zero-knowledge encryption for Drop, transient no-storage forwarding for Alias, and open source code
- **Private**: We minimize what exists at rest and keep file encryption keys client-side

## What We Offer

### Alias - Private Email Forwarding

Your email address is your digital identity. It's used to track you across the web, target you with ads, and link your accounts together.

With anon.li Alias, you can:
- Create email aliases that forward to your real inbox
- Reply from aliases without revealing your real address
- Block spam with one click by disabling an alias
- Add PGP encryption for end-to-end security
- Use custom domains for professional branding

### Drop - Encrypted File Sharing

Sharing sensitive files shouldn't mean trusting a third party. With anon.li Drop, your files are encrypted in your browser before they ever leave your device.

Features include:
- AES-256-GCM encryption with keys you control
- Automatic expiration and download limits
- Optional password protection
- Zero-knowledge storage - we literally cannot see your files
- Free account uploads with public link-only downloads

## Our Commitment

**Transparency**: Our platform code is [open source](https://codeberg.org/anonli/anon.li). Anyone can audit our code and verify our security claims.

**Minimal Data**: We collect only what's necessary to provide the service. No tracking, no analytics cookies, no selling data.

**Sustainability**: Free tiers are generous because privacy shouldn't depend on your wallet. Paid plans help us build a sustainable service for everyone.
